About Me

I'm currently a junior at CaliforniaInstitute of Technology (Caltech) studying mechanical engineering with a minor in Biology, and I am passionate about healthcare, systems design, and hands-on engineering. I was partially raised in Colombia, which shaped my sense of community, adaptability, and drive to make meaningful change. This perspective has stayed with me as I collaborate on projects or volunteer my time. Outside academics, I’m involved with various communities around campus that reflect my interests and values. I work with the Caltech Hispanic & Latino Association (CHLA) to help build community, play on the Caltech soccer team, and contribute to organizations like Engineers Without Borders, FSAE, and SWE. These experiences have taught me leardership and keep me grounded.

Professional Experience

I've had the opportunity to work across multiple engineering and research environments—from bioengineering to robotics and healthcare infrastructure.
