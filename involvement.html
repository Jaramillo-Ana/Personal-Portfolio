Involvements

Caltech Hispanic and Latino Association
Vice President
The Caltech Hispanic & Latino Association (CHLA) is a student organization and Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) chapter.
As vice president of the club, I help support our commitment to enhancing the Latino undergraduate community at Caltech.
As part of the Caltech community, I’ve been involved in various extracurricular activities ranging from volunteer work, campus jobs, and community engagement.

Engineers Without Borders
Secretary
Engineering Without Borders (EWB) is a Caltech student chapter. As secretary of the club, I organize events and meetings as well as support our current project: building a sustainable water system for a community in Ecuador.

Caltech Women's Soccer Team
DII College Athlete
Compete with Caltech’s Women Soccer Team under Division 3 soccer on the SCIAC.



Student Admission Ambassador
Student Worker
Work under Caltech’s Undergraduate Admissions Office to help facilitate staff to student interaction. I give tours, hold online sessions, blog, and work on the office.

ACEign Autism
Volunteer
As part of the ACEing Autism’s program at Caltech, I volunteer to bring tennis and a community to children with autism.



Society of Women's Engineers
Student Member
The Society of Women’s Engineers (SWE) chapter at Caltech focuses on enhancing the professional life of women engineers and I’ve taken part in all of their event.

Caltech Y
Academic Tutor
Volunteered with the Caltech Y to help offer tutoring to high school students from around the Pasadena-area. Tutored in Chemistry and Biology
